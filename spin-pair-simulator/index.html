Population kinetics and ODMR

Initializing simulator...

This browser model treats the defect as a closed kinetic network: optical pumping populates the excited singlet, ISC feeds the metastable triplet manifold, charge hopping generates spin-pair triplets, and recombination routes return population to the ground singlet. ODMR is estimated by comparing steady-state photoluminescence with microwave mixing disabled or enabled.

It is a population-level rate-equation model, not a full Hamiltonian or density-matrix treatment.
